Brief history of Michael Jackson


Michael Jackson was a legend who radiated love and preached unity through his music. He was a millenial gift to the world. His undisputed talent made him famous at a very young age but dedication to his passion for music engendered an unprecedented rise the entertainment industry had never witnessed prior to MJ's art.



He soon became the singer of what is still said to be the best selling album of all time -Thriller. Over the course of his career, he accepted 20 Guiness World Records including "Most Successful Entertainer Of All Time"


Let's browse through Michael Jackson's biography


It is impossible to completely know him but to get a glimpse of what his life was, we are going down memory lane, starting out where all living things do -birth.


  • 1958 -Born on the 29th of August to Katherine and Joe Jackson in Gary, Indiana.
  • 1962 -At the tender age of 4, he and his siblings formed a band called The Jackson 5.
  • 1968-The Jackson 5 band got signed by the renowned Motown.
  • 1969-Fame sweeps The Jackson 5 off their feet as their song "I Want You Back" takes number one singles spot.
  • 1971-Little Michael goes solo, releasing hits back to back, including "Got To Be There".
  • 1978-Now 20 years old, Michael Jackson delves deeper into his creative art as he explores acting in "The Wiz" with co-star Diana Ross.
  • 1979-Jackson records his first album as a solo artist "Off The Wall". The famous single "Don't Stop Till You Get Enough" sky-rocket to numbr one hits, as well as some other of his songs.
  • 1980-Hard work begins to pay off as he lands himself his first Grammy Award for "Best R&B Male Vocal Performance".
  • 1982-The best selling album of all time gets released by... you guessed who, The King Of Pop! This legendary hit tops the charts for 37 whole weeks! His signature move, the moonwalk, was seen for the first time in this album.
  • 1985-Lionel Richie nad Jackson pen "We Are The World" with the proceeds from sales of the single slated for hunger relief in Africa. Ray Charles, Stevie Wonder, Cyndi Lauper and other prominent artists lend their voices to the song. It sells a record seven million copies.
  • 1990-Guiness Book of World Records certifies Thriller as the "Best-selling Album Ever".
  • 1994-Love reaches MJ on the 26th of May as Lisa-Marie Presley walks down the aisle with him.
  • 1995-Sony releases HIStory: Past, Present and Future Book I. Janet Jackson performs a duet with her older brother on "Scream."
  • 1996-Jarvis Cocker of the British band Pulp accosts Jackson in mid-act at the BRIT Awards. Jackson was surrounded by children and a rabbi performing "Earth Song." Cocker claims Jackson had attempted to imitate Christ.
  • 1997-Following the heartbreak of a failed marriage, Jackson still chooses to give love another shot as he marries Debbie Rowe. They have a son named Prince Michael Jackson.
  • 1998-Little Prince Michael welcomes a sister, Paris Michael Katherine.
  • 2007-The King Of Pop and Sony buy Famous Music LLC from Viacom. This gives MJ rights to songs by some artistes including Shakira, Eminem, Beck and others.
  • 2008-Silver jubilee celebration is held for the legendary Thriller, for it's 25th anniversary. MJ reissues Thriller 25 which hits number 1 in 8 countries.
  • 2009-Black Thursday (25th June) as we lose a precious gem, a quintessential artiste, an advocate for love and peace, THE KING OF POP.
